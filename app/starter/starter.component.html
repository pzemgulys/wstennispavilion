Join us on Friday nights, March through May 2019, at SCORE Tennis Club in Countryside for a series of tennis drill mixer/auctions. These will start March 1, 2019, and help us build world-class Pavillion seating at Spring Rock Park in Western Springs.

USPTA pros, including Simon Piacente, Dave Evans, and Dan Koucky, and former junior director, Mark Hansen will be leading drills.
  • Refreshments are included in the ticket price.
  • A silent auction and an opportunity to sponsor a stadium seat (only 28 available) where your name will be permanently posted and live on in West Suburban tennis infamy, will be available too!
  • Minimum of $100 dollars in pledges for admission to "Hit-a-Thon""

Come to a fun-filled tennis drill/mixer/auction! In efforts to build a World-Class seating pavilion, we need the help of the entire tennis community to raise funds. We will be conducting fun-filled mixers/auctions to help promote this project.
Please help in our efforts to trailblaze a new future at Spring Rock Park.
